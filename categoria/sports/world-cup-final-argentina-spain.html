Argentina and Spain Set to Meet in the World Cup 2026 Final

The 2026 World Cup now has its two finalists: Argentina and Spain will face off on July 19 for the biggest title in international soccer.

VexlowHQ Staff·July 16, 2026·5 min

The 2026 World Cup now has its two finalists. Argentina and Spain will face off on July 19 to crown a new world champion, in a matchup that promises to be one of the most compelling in years.

After a pair of thrilling semifinals, both national teams overcame their rivals and will now fight to lift the most important trophy in international soccer.

Argentina returns to a World Cup final

Argentina's national team sealed its spot after beating England 2-1 in a match decided in the final minutes. After falling behind early, La Albiceleste responded with character and turned the score around to secure another place in the tournament's decider.

With this result, Argentina keeps alive the dream of defending the title it won in the previous edition and adding another star to its history.

Spain arrives with authority

Spain booked its ticket to the final by beating France 2-0 in a rock-solid performance. The Spanish side dominated large stretches of the match and confirmed the high level it has shown throughout the World Cup.

The team coached by Luis de la Fuente will look to win its second World Cup and write another golden chapter in Spanish soccer history.

A final steeped in history

The Argentina-Spain matchup brings together two of the tournament's most talented squads. On one side, the Albiceleste's experience and pedigree; on the other, the dynamic, attacking soccer Spain has shown throughout the competition.

Millions of fans around the world will be watching a match that will crown the 2026 World Cup champion and promises drama from start to finish.

A third-place match too

Before the grand final, France and England will face off to decide third place in the tournament. Both teams will look to close out their run with a win after being eliminated in the semifinals.

Upcoming World Cup 2026 matches

  • Third-place match: France vs. England — July 18, 2026
  • Grand Final: Argentina vs. Spain — July 19, 2026

The world awaits a new champion

With just one match left to play, the 2026 World Cup enters its final chapter. Argentina will try to defend its crown, while Spain looks to return to the top of world soccer. Everything is set for a final that will be remembered in World Cup history.
