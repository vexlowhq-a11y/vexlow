GTA 6: Rockstar Keeps the Mystery Alive as Fans Wait for Trailer 3

With GTA VI just months away from launch, Rockstar has gone quiet on the third trailer as theories about a possible August announcement grow.

VexlowHQ Staff·July 17, 2026·7 min

With Grand Theft Auto VI just months from launch, the gaming community is watching Rockstar Games' every move. Pre-orders are already open and the release date remains locked in for November 19, 2026, but one big question remains: when will the long-awaited Trailer 3 arrive?

Rockstar's silence is fueling theories

Rockstar Games runs a marketing playbook very different from other studios. After the second trailer and the opening of pre-orders, the company has largely gone quiet.

That lack of information has sent thousands of players combing through every post, every update to the official site, and even Take-Two Interactive's upcoming earnings events, looking for any clue about a new announcement.

August could be a key month

One of the most talked-about topics in the community is Take-Two's upcoming shareholder meeting, set for early August.

Many players believe Rockstar could use that moment to show the first real gameplay footage of GTA 6, or drop the official third trailer.

For now, there's no official confirmation, but interest is enormous, and social media is already full of theories about a possible announcement in the coming weeks.

Vice City is shaping up to be Rockstar's biggest world yet

While Rockstar keeps guarding plenty of secrets, it has confirmed that GTA 6 will take players back to Vice City, inside the fictional state of Leonida, inspired by Florida.

The map will include:

  • Major cities.
  • Beaches and coastal areas.
  • Swamps.
  • Small towns.
  • Long stretches of highway.
  • Numerous side activities.

Everything points to this being the most ambitious open world Rockstar has ever built.

Jason and Lucia will be the leads

For the first time in the main series' history, GTA will feature a two-protagonist story.

Players will control:

  • Jason Duval
  • Lucia Caminos

The story will revolve around both characters as they pull off heists, chases, and survive within Leonida's criminal underworld.

Rockstar has described the story as inspired by the Bonnie and Clyde dynamic, with trust between the two characters as a core part of the narrative.

The launch is already making a historic impact

Anticipation for GTA 6 is so massive that companies in the United States have started preparing special plans for launch day.

Some companies have jokingly acknowledged expecting a major dip in employee attendance on November 19, as thousands of people plan to spend the day exclusively playing.

Several publishers have also shifted their own release dates to avoid going head-to-head with Rockstar's launch.

Is a PC version coming too?

One of the most repeated questions is still about a PC release.

Rockstar has so far only confirmed versions for:

  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox Series X|S

The PC edition still has no official date.

Former Rockstar developers have explained that the studio's priority has always been optimizing console versions first before working on the PC adaptation — a strategy it already used with GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2.

The countdown has begun

With pre-orders open and the release date confirmed, GTA 6 is entering the final stretch of its marketing campaign.

All eyes are now on Rockstar Games and the possibility that the studio will drop a new trailer showing full gameplay for the first time.

If that happens, it could become one of the most-watched videos of the year in the entertainment industry.
